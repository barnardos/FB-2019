Overview for 2019

We’ve worked on 47 projects in 2019, in these four stages of development:  1. Discovery  |  2. Alpha  |  3. Beta  |  4. Live

1. Discovery

We've conducted 13 discovery projects to research a problem space and identify user needs:

How can we best use technology to support central business services?

What content do our users want to find on children’s service website pages?

How can we better support young people as they move on from care?

How can we tackle loneliness and isolation in care leavers?

Can we design better sex and relationship education for young people?

How can we improve our network connectivity?

How can we better use data to inform our decision making?

How can we use innovation to collect GiftAid and donor data in a smart way?

How can we share information and collaborate better with sector influencers and partners?

How can we assess risk or identify sexual abuse in a more child-centred way?

How can we have better conversations with children about mental health?

How can we improve our communication, onboarding and offboarding of children and young people to our services?

How can we co-design services with children and young people?
