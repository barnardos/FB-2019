#FutureBarnardos

The #FutureBarnardos programme helps transform Barnardo’s by designing and delivering the next generation of children’s health and social care, together with children, young people and professionals across the sector.

We work to achieve this change through three pillars:

  1. Transforming technology for all our colleagues, so that we work more efficiently and confidently
  2. Transforming data for practitioners and leaders, so that we make more effective decisions about helping young people
  3. Transforming services, so that we provide the tools and information children, families, colleagues and supporters need most

We have seen tangible results from our work influencing, designing and leading change in health and social care:

  • designing and sharing more efficient ways of working
  • attracting funding and support from external partners who see the value of our work
  • producing tools which meet the standards and expectations of the digital age

We’re making changes to empower us all to be better at collaborating, working smarter and bringing new ideas to life. We work in the open, with young people, families, experts, practitioners, commissioners and partners, within and beyond Barnardo’s.

We take a human-centred design approach so that everything, from face-to-face services to our website, gives the support children and families need, in the way they need it.

Barnardo’s digital transformation is built on three fundamental principles:

  1. We put children and young people’s voices and needs at the heart of everything we do
  2. We make decisions based on evidence
  3. We start small and iterate towards big goals

The #FutureBarnardos team are proud to be rebellious, challenging and disruptive. We champion diversity, data, and the power of tech for good. These values inspire us to make Barnardo’s work even better for the children and young people we support today and the success stories they’ll become tomorrow.

We are proud to share everything we do. Follow our progress on #FutureBarnardos blog and #FutureBarnardos website.

Jason Caplin,

Director, Digital and Technology, Barnardo’s
