Robust governance and compliance

We make sure all our work meets business needs and is aligned to our corporate strategy.

Our projects are governed by Barnardo’s Corporate Programme Board (CPB), Corporate Leadership Team (CLT) or senior steering groups. We report routinely to FinCom and the Audit and Risk Committee.

This year we have met some of Barnardo’s most urgent compliance needs, including GDPR compliance, NHS Toolkit compliance, ISO27001 and passing the Gambling Commission audit required to continue Barnardo’s lottery activities.

We have piloted the Be The Change programme, involving colleagues from across Barnardo’s to agree the priorities we should work on to create better outcomes for more children. We will implement Be the Change in full in 2020 as a key part of our governance structure.
